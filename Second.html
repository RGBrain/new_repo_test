Follow these instructions to make a great sandwich

  1. Buy some lovely bread - I recommend sourdough!
  2. Cut yourself a couple of nice think slices
  3. Butter both, generously
  4. Add fillings, I would suggest bacon, brie and cranberry, but you do you!
  5. Salt & pepper. Always.
  6. Cut your sandwich in half, triangles - you're worth it.
